What is a disk image? A copy of whatever data you have at the time of creation.

What is the command to make a disk image? The command is dd if=dev/zero bs=1M
 count=40000 >> diskimage.img

What is a disk partition? A hard disk partition is a defined storage space
on a hard drive. 

How many disk partitions are in a disk image? It can depend on what you want
to copy. 

What difficulties does this pose if any? The size of the amount of partitions
can be difficult to deal with especially if you make a disk image of all
of your computer files.

What steps must you take to work with this? The step you must take to work 
with this is to only copy the data most important to you so the disk size
isn't overbearing.
